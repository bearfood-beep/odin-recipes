Amazing Tacos

Woah, how did tacos get so good?

Ingredients

  • meat of your choice
  • taco shell
  • spicy
  • shredded lettuce
  • cheese
  • onion
  • lime
  • avacado sauce

    • steps

    • get in your car, dont forget wallet
    • go to nearest taco truck or mexican restraunt
    • wait in line to be served
    • tell them you want tacos with the above ingredents
    • pay them
    • wait 5 to 10 minutes
    • get the tacos
    • sit down
    • squeeze lime juice on tacos
    • spread avacado sauce on tacos
    • eat them
    • sit back and reflect on their deliciousness!!
